> Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Do you have a minimum age requirement?

A: Yes, all members need to be over the age of 18.

Q: What time zone do you fly?

A: We operate on Eastern Standard Time (EST).

Q: What airframes do you support?

A: We welcome F/A-18C, F-16C, F-15E, F-15C and A-10C pilots.

Q: How can I join?

A: Join our Discord server by clicking the Discord logo.
